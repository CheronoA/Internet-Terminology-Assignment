🏢 Intranet

An intranet is a private network using internet technologies (HTTP, HTML) accessible only to members of an organization. It is used for internal communication, document sharing, HR systems, and collaboration tools.

🔒 Key Characteristics

  • Private and restricted to organization members only
  • Not accessible from the public internet without VPN
  • Protected by firewalls and authentication systems
  • Uses same technologies as the public web

📋 Common Intranet Uses

  • Company news and announcements
  • Employee directories and profiles
  • Document management and file sharing
  • HR portals (leave requests, payroll, benefits)
  • Internal wikis and knowledge bases

🆚 Intranet vs Extranet

🏢 Intranet
Internal employees only
Behind corporate firewall
🤝 Extranet
Employees + authorized outsiders
Secure portal with login
